About Us

Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc. has been a trusted leader in providing innovative radiology solutions since 1988. Based in Gresham, Oregon, we specialize in delivering high-quality portable X-ray systems, radiation shielding apparel, and medical supplies for healthcare professionals across the globe.
